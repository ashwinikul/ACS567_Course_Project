Welcome to Know Thyself Website

The Myers-Briggs Type Indicator instrument is the most administered and most recognized personality type tool in the world.Based on the model by Carl Jung and made manifest by Isabel Myers, the MBTI tool provides a framework for understanding one's way of both collecting information and making decisions to allow for maximizing personal and interpersonal effectiveness.

After more than 50 years of development, the MBTI instrument continues to be one of the leading personality assessment tools for:
1- Understanding individual differences
2- Team-building - uncovering new ways to work and interact with others
3- Executive coaching and management training
4- Performance management - improving individual and team performance
5- Retaining top talent
6- Developing effective leadership skills
7- Reducing workplace conflict and enhancing teamwork
8- Career assessment, career development and mid-career change

The Basic Myers-Briggs Model of Personality Type All of us have four kinds of mental functions which we use to collect information (the Perceiving functions) and to make decisions (the Judging functions). These functions are both processed internally (Introverted) and externally - shown to the outside world (Extroverted).

The two Perceiving functions are:
- SENSING (S):focusing on the elements in the present, clarifying facts, cataloging information, identifying physical responses
- INTUITION (N): sharing ideas, exploring possibilities, making connections, looking into the future, planning ahead

The two Judging functions are:
- THINKING (T): critiquing the data, the reasons and the logic; analyzing if/then, creating a model
- FEELING (F):connecting with others, centering on values, clarifying personal values, evaluating worth
